Welcome to Analytic Cube



Analytic Cube is a company in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Canada, that specializes in healthcare analytics. We formed this company out of a growing need for machine learning and artificial intelligence in healthcare applications. We are currently partnering with big data researchers from a local university, clinical researchers from a local university teaching hospital, and local investors with backgrounds in accounting and finance, to build a healthcare analytics platform which can improve the quality of healthcare, such as reducing surgical wait times.




Analytic Cube has already completed a couple of pilot projects. The first project involved working with a surgical wait times dataset, and utilized multiple linear regression to make predictions on surgical wait times based on surgical specialty. This pilot project culminated in the development of a web application for public consumption: Analysis of Surgical Wait Times in Nova Scotia. We met with the Department of Health and currently awaiting commissioning of the project to develop it further.




The second project involved working with air pollution datasets, and utilized data mining, data warehousing, and visualization to perform detailed analytics on air pollution levels in different regions, as air pollution is detrimental to health. This pilot project culminated in the development of an interactive dashboard for public consumption: Analysis of Air Pollution in Nova Scotia. We met with the Department of Environment and currently awaiting commissioning of the project to develop it further.




Currently, we are working on two projects: the first one is a doctor appointment booking application, and the second one is an AI app, which classifies sleep apnea based on clinical sleep polysomnography data, utilizing biomedical sensor processing, machine learning, and deep learning.

To learn more about Analytic Cube and about how our healthcare analytics platform can help your clinical practice and healthcare organization, please contact us for a demonstration.
